0.Selecting Importing¶

We have selected the SPDR Gold Shares (GLD) ETF as the gold ETF. It is traded on Nasdaq and the currency is USD Similarly, we have selected the Hang Seng Investment Index Funds Series II - Hang Seng Index ETF as the equity ETF. It is a market cap weighted equity ETF traded on Heng Seng stock exchange. The currency is HKD. Data source: https://finance.yahoo.com/
